Favorite things

Here are some of my favorite products that my kids love and that make my life easier:

Magnetic Tiles

You can purchase the name brand Magna Tiles, but we love these. They are the Picasso brand and they are sturdy and hold up really well! And, they are way more affordable. Keeps my kids occupied for hours!

FORT

This is unfortunately sold out. It was a brilliant idea! There are similar products out there, like the Nugget.

Water Table

Any basic water table will do. But be prepared for your kids to have an absolute blast! Don't spend a ton of money on an elaborate one. They will be happy to just be outside in the water!

Dollhouse

This is the dollhouse we got for our kids. Again, nothing too expensive or elaborate, but it keeps them busy. Even our older boys will play with it. It's a hit!
