Feeding Kids

Figuring out what to feed all of the humans in your house for three meals a day for the rest of your life? Sounds fun!

I used to get excited thinking about cooking for my family. The thought of making nutritious and delicious meals excited me! I love to cook! Now? It's probably my least facorite thing to do. my kids are picky. I have to pull teeth to get my kids to even lick a vegetable. My goal though is to make sure they are eating some sort of plant at least two meals a day. It doesn't matter if it's a fruit or a vegatable. If it is made of plant cells, it's a win!


Something that experts might not recommend, but I highly recommend, is hiding the vegetables. Chocolate zucchini bread is a win in my book! I have also blended up beets in their pancakes and made "pink" pancakes. I'm such a fun mom!!


The honest truth is that if you are at least trying and are concerned, you're a good parent. If your kids are still alive because you're giving them enough food, you're a good parent.

Here is one of my favorite recipes. Not too much sugar, packed full of zucchini, and my kids gobble it right up. Chocolate Chip Zucchini Bread (cake)
