Activities

I'm not that kind of mom that sits and plays with her kids all day. I like them to entertain themselves. But one activity I love to do when I just want to lie down is called "What's On My Butt?"

Here's the jist. I lie down on my belly, the kids put something on my bottom. I can't look. Then I have to guess what it is.

That's it! Easy as pie! They love it, I love it. It's a win-win.

 


About Me
